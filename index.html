What Is Social Engineering?

Social engineering is the art of manipulating people to give up confidential information like passwords, social security number/PPS and bank login details.

Criminals use social engineering tactics because it is usually easier to exploit your trust than it is to discover ways to hack your software and steal your information as it would take an extreme amount of time and manpower.
