What is social engeeniring?

Social engineering is the art of manipulating people to give up confidential information like passwords, social security number/PPS and bank login details.

Criminals use social engineering tactics to exploit your trust to get the information than to hack into the website to steal your information as it would take an extreme amount of time and manpower.
